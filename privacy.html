Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you. This includes your:

  • Name
  • Contact information
  • Interactions with our site
We collect this information to:
  • Contact or respond to customers using the contact-us or Reservations forms.
  • Distribute Haukai Restaurant newsletters and updates (optional).
  • Gather analytical information about the websites use to improve the user experience.
Providing this information is optional.
  • Our contact number is available as an alternative to submitting contact-us and Reservation forms.
  • Cookies are optional, if you don’t allow cookies we can’t improve the user experience of your device. Alternatively, contact-us to lodge a bug or improvement.
Your information is stored securely and will not be shared outside of these points.

You have the right to:
  • Ask for a copy of any personal information we have about you.
  • Ask for it to be corrected if it’s wrong.
To ask for a copy your personal information, or to correct it, contact-us at admin@haukai.co.nz.

This is in compliance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020
https://www.privacy.org.nz/privacy-act-2020/privacy-principles
